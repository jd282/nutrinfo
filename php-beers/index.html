Group Bio

We are a group that formed in October 2016 after an in-class mixer. We have Ademola Olayinka (Ademolition), Nick Ngare (NickNick), Jennifer Du (JenJen), and Bao Doan (BaoBao). We thought of the wonderful idea of getting all of our food information in one place since a lot of us are into our health and fitness. We are working closely with Student Affairs at Duke University to make this a possiblity. Student health ain't gave us shit.
